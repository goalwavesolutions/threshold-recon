Threshold revenue reconciliation — five-day target state

Subledger architecture; integrated stack: NetSuite, Numeric, Stripe, Hex

Pre-closeLive data ingest
Day 1Cutoff & subledger lock
Day 2Auto-post; flux opens
Day 3Numeric review queue
Day 4Revenue BS recs
Day 5Lock & sign-off
Systems
  • Daily ingest: Stripe + platform DB → revenue subledger
  • Live MTD revenue view
  • Auto-flag unmatched invoice ↔ payment
  • Clean dataset to Hex for revenue analytics
  • Cutoff after review of closing month’s revenue data
  • Revenue exceptions documented; estimates proposed for unintegrated gaps
  • Revenue subledger locked; JEs created systemically
  • Source JEs auto-post to NetSuite (post sign-off)
  • Estimate JEs reviewed and posted
  • Revenue flux review opens (MoM, vs F/B, by product)
  • Numeric pulls posted revenue JEs for review
  • Flux-driven JEs prepped and posted by EOD
  • Deferred revenue, AR, and contract liability recs finalised in Numeric
  • Period locked
  • Revenue workpapers archived
AI
  • Analytical layer above subledger (not in the match path)
  • Anomaly monitoring on revenue volumes and values
  • Daily/weekly insight: revenue by product
  • Orchestrates revenue close — kicks off workflow
  • Messages accountant; runs revenue checklist
  • Analytical tooling to support human estimates
  • Drafts revenue flux commentary on large/unusual items
  • Initial revenue cut for stakeholders
  • Auto-notifies controller on material revenue JEs
  • Continued orchestration: reminders, status
  • Continued revenue flux drafting
  • Drafts deferred revenue and AR commentary
  • Drafts revenue management reporting cut
  • Final revenue completeness and exception sweep
  • Documents post-close feedback for next period
Human
  • Light-touch on revenue close plumbing
  • Business partnering — surfacing revenue insight to wider org
  • Continuous improvement of crosswalk and exception rules
  • Steps in only on AI escalation
  • Sr accountant: runs orchestrated revenue workflow, escalates unusual items
  • Controller: sense-checks revenue KPIs, early signal on top-line
  • Sr accountant: reviews/approves revenue estimate JEs, works flagged flux
  • Controller: owns revenue variance narrative, pressure-tests AI calls
  • Sr accountant: clears Numeric queue, evidence, flux JEs
  • Controller: signs off recs, approves flux JEs, shapes revenue close package
  • Sr accountant: finalises deferred revenue and AR recs, evidence in Numeric
  • Controller: edits revenue commentary, MR review
  • Controller: period sign-off — the assertion AI doesn’t make
  • Joint post-close retro and actions
  • Sr accountant: archive completeness check
Systems — automated, integrated stack
AI — orchestration, drafting, analytics
Human — judgment, sign-off, business partnering

Scope: revenue reconciliation workflow only. Other close streams — expense accruals, payroll, balance sheet items outside the revenue cycle, consolidation — sit alongside on the same calendar but are out of scope for this design. The same orchestration pattern generalises.
