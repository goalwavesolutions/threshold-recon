  • Daily/weekly insight: revenue by product, spend by category

  • Analytical tooling to support estimates and accruals
  • Drafts flux commentary across revenue and expense

Human

  • Business partnering — surfacing insight from live revenue and spend data
  • Continuous process improvement (rules, taxonomy)

  • Controller: sense-checks high-level KPIs, early signal to wider team
  • Sr accountant: reviews/approves revenue estimate JEs, works flagged revenue flux
  • FP&A: picks up expense accrual review (handoff)
  • Controller: owns variance narrative, pressure-tests AI calls
  • Sr accountant: clears revenue Numeric queue, evidence, flux JEs
  • FP&A: expense flux narrative, budget-owner engagement
  • Controller: signs off recs and approves flux JEs; reviews AI-drafted CFS; shapes close package
  • Sr accountant: revenue BS recs, evidence in Numeric
  • FP&A: completes expense flux commentary

  • Joint post-close retro and actions
  • Sr accountant: archive completeness check
Systems — automated, integrated stack
AI — orchestration, drafting, analytics
Human — judgment, sign-off, business partnering

Outside the 5-day critical path: payroll JEs (post mid-month following calc and approval); stock-based compensation (external admin schedule); monthly tax provision stub with quarterly true-up; cap table and equity activity (event-driven). Each follows its own cadence and is reflected in GL ahead of period close.

Automated upstream of the close: depreciation and fixed asset roll-forward, prepaid amortization, lease accounting (ASC 842), FX translation where applicable. These flow as scheduled JEs through the pre-close ingest with exception-only human review.

Conditional on entity structure: consolidation and intercompany eliminations would layer in on Days 2–3 (Systems-led, Controller review) where the operating model includes multiple legal entities.
